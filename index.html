Our story

We got the inspiration to build Delicious Days by Kamome Diner, a movie that follows a Japanese woman who sets up a diner serving Japanese food in Helsinki, Finland, and the friends she makes in the process. The whole journey is healing, blissful and endearing. We want to bring you the same experiences: to eat well, to connect the culture and people and to be happy.
